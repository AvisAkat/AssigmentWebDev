MAWULI SCHOOL

Welcome to Mawuli School

Mawuli school is a great school and a school of choice for that matter. The true identity of many prominent people started from here. The school has over the years turned out thousands of students who are occupying enviable positions in Ghana and beyond. The products of Mawuli School are top notch professionals in their various fields of endeavour. They cut across all disciplines of study, such as, Medicine, Engineering, Architecture, Politics, Teaching, Business and many more. Indeed, students who passed through Mawuli School are embodied with the motto: Head, Heart, Hand. And also carry with them an uncommon Grace and Favour to do well in any field of speciality. Mawuli School is undoubtedly a fine school to have attended and an awesome place to behold.

Special Events

Homecoming Event
Mawuli School recently hosted a massive homecoming event for its alumni. This event, known as the 67th Congress and Homecoming of the Old Mawuli Students Union (OMSU), saw thousands of past students returning to the school. The weekend was filled with nostalgia as alumni dressed in their old school uniforms, participated in an inter-house cooking competition, and enjoyed a borborbor music night.

75th Anniversary Launch
Mawuli School is gearing up for its 75th anniversary next year, and the celebrations were officially launched during the homecoming event. The anniversary launch included various activities such as a church service and an all-white ceremony.
